The Straw Hat Crew

The legendary pirates who sail under the Jolly Roger, seeking adventure and treasure!

Monkey D. Luffy

Bounty: 1.5 Billion Berries

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. His goal is to become the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure known as One Piece.

Roronoa Zoro

Bounty: 60 Million Berries

Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. He aims to become the greatest swordsman in the world by defeating Dracule Mihawk.

Nami

Bounty: 66 Million Berries

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She dreams of drawing a complete map of the world.

Usopp

Bounty: 30 Million Berries

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a master of long-range combat and skilled at creating gadgets.

Sanji

Bounty: 77 Million Berries

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. He dreams of finding the All Blue, a legendary sea where all the seas meet.

Tony Tony Chopper

Bounty: 100 Berries

Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit, giving him the ability to transform into a human.

Nico Robin

Bounty: 130 Million Berries

Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. She seeks to uncover the true history of the world, including the Void Century.

Franky

Bounty: 94 Million Berries

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates. He built the crew's ship, the Thousand Sunny, and is a cyborg with incredible strength.

Brook

Bounty: 83 Million Berries

Brook is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a living skeleton who ate the Revive-Revive Fruit, allowing him to come back to life.

Jinbe

Bounty: 400 Million Berries

Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a fish-man and a master of Fish-Man Karate, with a deep sense of loyalty and honor.
